About Me

Engineer by Degree.
Technician by Nature.

My engineering journey began long before college, fueled by a lifelong obsession with cars and a drive to learn by doing. From my first car — a 1995 BMW 325i I've owned for over seven years and driven 60,000+ miles — to full engine and transmission swaps, I've rebuilt nearly every major vehicle system by hand.

That hands-on foundation pushed me to pursue Mechanical Engineering at UIC, where I graduated in May 2024. I now bring both analytical rigor and practical skill to every challenge — from ANSYS finite element analysis to MIG welding and chassis fabrication.

I'm seeking a role where real-world problem-solving matters as much as simulation results.
